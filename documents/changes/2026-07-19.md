## What Changed

### 1) Pre-order Tracker — scrollable lists and quantity/price detail per row

In `components/purchase/preorder-tracker.tsx`:

- The "All orders" list (Pending / Ready for pickup / Collected) had no height limit, so once there were more than a handful of pre-orders the whole page grew very long. It's now capped and scrolls inside its own box (using the same themed scrollbar as the Catalog page), so the tabs/search/sort bar and the bulk-select bar stay put while you scroll the list underneath.
- The "Ready for pickup at {shop}" item lists got the same treatment — the item list scrolls, but the shop's total row stays pinned below it so it's always visible.
- Each row in "All orders" only ever showed the total price, not how it was made up. When quantity is more than 1, rows now also show a `×N` pill next to the total and the per-unit price in the subtitle line (e.g. "Regular · Normal · RM 25.00/unit"), so it's clear how the total was derived instead of just seeing one lump-sum figure.

## User Impact

- The Pre-order Tracker page stays a manageable height even with many pending orders — no more endless scrolling through the whole page to reach the bottom summary or navigation.
- At a glance, it's clear whether a pre-order's total reflects one unit or several, and what each unit costs.
