## Quick Overview

Added a **Total pre-orders** summary card to the web Pre-order Tracker, so the
overall count of pending pre-orders is visible at a glance alongside the
existing Awaiting / Ready / Deadline / Balance due cards.

Files touched:

- `components/purchase/preorder-tracker.tsx`

## What Changed

### 1. Total pre-orders summary card

The Pre-order Tracker's summary row now starts with a **Total pre-orders** card.
It shows the number of pre-order line items still in the pipeline (not yet
collected) — i.e. Awaiting production + Ready for pickup. The summary grid was
widened from 4 to 5 columns on large screens to fit it.

**Why:** the tracker previously showed the breakdown (awaiting, ready) and the
money owed, but not a single headline "how many pre-orders do I have" number.
This mirrors the same addition made to the mobile app on the same day.

## User Impact

- On the Pre-order Tracker, users immediately see the **total pending
  pre-orders** count, not just the per-stage breakdown.

## Notes for Next Time

- "Total pre-orders" counts **line items** not yet collected, to stay
  consistent with the tracker's other item-count cards. The dashboard's
  pre-order figures count **units** (quantities) — a deliberate, internally
  consistent difference between the two screens.
